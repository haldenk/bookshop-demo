Cookie Policy

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit certain web pages. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the owners of the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to store your consent preferences regarding the use of tracking technologies associated with Mautic, a marketing automation platform. Specifically, we use cookies to remember whether you have accepted or rejected the use of tracking cookies for analytics and marketing purposes, this is our only use of cookies.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Functional Cookies: These cookies are essential for the operation of our Website and enable us to provide you with basic functionality, such as remembering your cookie consent preferences.
  • On this site, cookies will only store the "accepted" or "rejected" key. No personal information will be stored in the cookie.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways.

  • Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. You can usually find these settings in the "options" or "preferences" menu of your browser.
  • Site footer In the footer of the site, If you have accepted cookies you will see a button to remove cookies. This will set the cookie value to "rejected" and any tracking will be denied. Adversly, if you have denied cookies, you will see an "allow cookies" button enable them.

    5. Third-Party Cookies

    We use cookies associated with Mautic, a third-party marketing automation platform, for tracking purposes. These cookies are hosted on a domain separate from our own but are essential for providing analytics and marketing functionality.
